“I Can Eat Boogers Too” by Daniel Prokop | BOOK COVER DESIGN, ILLUSTRATION

I was tasked with redesigning a book cover for “I Can Eat Boogers Too!”, a humorous collection of stories about children and all the funny, awkward, and gross moments that come with them.

The new cover needed a whimsical style that appealed to parents, while still feeling light and playful. I incorporated colorful, organic elements to reflect the humor of the content, while ensuring the overall design had a mature, polished look to suit the target audience.

Kaiju Energy | PACKAGING DESIGN

I was tasked with designing a bold new look for Kaiju Energy, a concept inspired by Japanese culture and aimed at a U.S. market with a love for Japanese pop culture. The goal was to create packaging that celebrates iconic symbols like the Japanese flag and Godzilla while appealing to an energy drink’s primarily masculine audience.

I focused on a striking palette of red, white, and black, blending simplicity with strong visual impact. The design is both dynamic and fresh, breaking away from typical energy drink tropes like lightning icons. The result is a bottle that’s eye-catching and memorable, capturing the essence of “Kaiju” and Japan in a way that appeals to the brand’s target audience.
